UCC College Enrollment

Data Privacy

1. I hereby authorize UNIDA CHRISTIAN COLLEGES to collect, use, process, and share, in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012, any personal and sensitive information I have provided upon registration and enrollment to the college for any college-related processes and researches, provided that my anonymity is kept confidential.

I hereby give my consent to Unida Christian Colleges for the collection and processing my personal data, relating to my application for admission, in accordance with the UCC Data Privacy notice for College Enrollment for Admission to Unida Christian Colleges, and in compliance with Republic Act 10173 or the Data Privacy Act of 2012 of the Republic of the Philippines, it's implementing Rules and Regulations, other guidelines and issuances by the National Privacy Commission.
